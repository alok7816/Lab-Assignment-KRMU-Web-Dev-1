Famous Monuments of India

The India Gate is a war memorial located in New Delhi, built to honor the soldiers of the Indian Army who lost their lives during World War I.

The Red Fort (Lal Qila) in Delhi is a historic fort that was the main residence of the Mughal emperors for nearly 200 years.

The Taj Mahal in Agra is a white marble mausoleum built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, and is regarded as a masterpiece of Mughal architecture.
